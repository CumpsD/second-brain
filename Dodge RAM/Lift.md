# Lift

## About

[Revel Performance - 2019-2022+ RAM Adjustable Air Suspension Links](https://www.revelperformance.com/lifts/2019-ram-adjustable-air-leveling-links-set-of-four-links/)

> Revel's Adjustable Air Suspension Links replace the stock sensor links to alter the ride height as much as 2.5", while maintaining functionality of the truck's suspension modes. The links utilize the factory ball studs for easy installation and require no alterations or cutting. Don't like the look? Totally cool. Revel Links can be easily removed at any time.
