# Tail Light LED Bar

## About

[Putco 9201960-04 - 60" Blade Red/Amber/White LED Tailgate Light Bar](https://www.putco.com/product/blade-tailgate-light-bar-plug-n-play/v/Dodge/Ram%201500/2019/)

> Give your vehicle some added light on its tailgate with the Putco Blade LED tailgate light bar. This industry-leading tailgate light bar comes in many different sizes and styles. Choose between 18″, 48″, and 60″, that output red, white, or amber. Our light bars can have more than 2000 LED’s in them depending on what size you choose from. LED’s are protected by tinted polycarbonate that is waterproof. The Blade is simple to install using a 16 gauge 4 pin wiring harness. Once installed, our light bar will operate perfectly with your running light, brake lights, hazard lights, and even your vehicles turn signals.
